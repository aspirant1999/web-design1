CAKE is a form of sweet food made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients, that is usually baked. In their oldest forms, cakes were modifications of bread, but cakes now cover a wide range of preparations that can be simple or elaborate, and that share features with other desserts such as pastries, meringues, custards, and pies.

Ingredients required :

  • NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • NESTLÉ a+ nourish Dahi
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Maida ( Flour)
  • Almonds, ground (Almond Flour)
  • Baking Powder
  • Vanilla Essence
  • Almond Flakes
  • NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Lime Juice
  • Fresh Cream

Steps you need to follow :

  • Preheat oven 🔥 to 180⁰ C. Lightly grease a loaf baking tin and dust with flour
  • Sieve together maida, Almond flour and baking powder. Keep aside.
  • Soften butter in a bowl, add NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk and beat well to a creamy consistency. Add vanilla essence and mix. Slowly beat in the eggs one by one, then fold in the sieved maida mixture and mix well. Add Nestlé a+ nourish Dahi and mix to get a dropping consistency.

  • Pour into the prepared loaf tin. Bake in the preheated oven for 40 -45 v minutes ⏱️ or till tooth pick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.

  • Remove from oven, cool for a while. Turn out over a wire rack or plate. Spread lemon icing on top and sprinkle Almond flakes.
  • Lemon🍋 Icing❄️: Place NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk in a bowl, add lime juice and cream. Beat well till thick. Pour over the cake and spread.🎂
